who are we?

We are GLITCH at UCLA, UCLA's premier hackathon club. Our club is by developers for developers: we build projects non-stop for people of all levels and experience. Our club aims to build any idea into a product and develop club members to compete and win hackathons.

Hackathon debut

Our teams will debut at LAHacks, and will be competing with teams from top universities to win prizes. We have team matching in our discord if interested.

50+ Active Members

We've grown to over 50 active members from diverse backgrounds, making us one of the largest tech communities at UCLA.

5+ Active Projects

We are currently working on 5+ active projects, many of which have been recognized at national competitions and industry events.
