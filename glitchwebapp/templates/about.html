about GLITCH

We're a community of builders, dreamers, and innovators at UCLA dedicated to making technology more accessible and fun for everyone.

our mission

01 Accessible Hackathons

Make hackathons more accessible to everyone regardless of experience level or background.

02 Open Environment

Foster a more open environment for everyone to work on passion projects with support and encouragement.

03 No Constraints

Build cool side projects without all the constraints that other organizations typically impose.

04 Uniqueness

Create a club that addresses all of the problems of other UCLA CS clubs by focusing on community and creativity.
