frequently asked questions

Do I need previous experience to join?
Not at all! GLITCH welcomes members of all skill levels. Whether you're just starting your coding journey or you're an experienced developer, there's a place for you in our community.
How do I get involved after joining?
After joining our Discord, introduce yourself and check out our project channels. We have regular meetings and project groups you can join based on your interests.
Are there any membership fees?
No, membership is completely free! We believe in making tech accessible to everyone.
How much time commitment is expected?
GLITCH is flexible! You can participate as much or as little as your schedule allows. Some members join specific project teams with regular meetings, while others drop in for occasional events.
